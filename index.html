PROJECT OVERVIEW

100 markets and millions of variations

For over a decade, we have been working on one of the largest digital sales projects in the automotive industry: the global vehicle configurator. It arose from the challenge of reconciling millions of options with complex, country-specific regulations and requirements, and has evolved into a central sales tool for end customers, dealers, and internal systems.

Our solution combines high scalability with absolute precision: automated data flows, integrated trading platforms, uniform pricing, and quality control for all product lines. This makes maximum complexity invisible and vehicle configuration as simple as a click.

Today, the configurator is used in over 100 markets. It transforms data into expertise, complexity into clarity – and forms the basis for an optimal digital customer experience.
